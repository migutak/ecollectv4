Portfolio at Risk

Summary

Portfolio at risk = Unpaid principal balance of all loans with late payments + renegotiated loans / Gross loan portfolio

Arrears rate = Amount past due/Gross loan portfolio

Repayment rate = Amount received (current+past due) less prepayments / (total payments due this period + amounts due from past periods)

Current recovery rate = Amount received this period (P or P+I)/Amount due this period(P or P+I) under original loan terms
